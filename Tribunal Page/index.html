Wolfgang Amadeus Mozart

Prolific and influential composer of the Classical period

Little bit of Wolfgang Amadeus Mozart's life:

  • 1756 - Born in Salzburg, Australia
  • 1762–73 - Travel. While Wolfgang was young, his family made several European journeys in which he and Nannerl performed as child prodigies.
  • 1773–77 - Employment at the Salzburg court as a court musician by the ruler of Salzburg, Prince-Archbishop Hieronymus Colloredo.
  • 1777–78 - Journey to Paris. Mozart ventured out once more in search of employment, with visits to Augsburg, Mannheim, Paris, and Munich.
  • 1781 - Mozart's opera Idomeneo premiered with "considerable success" in Munich.
  • 1782 - Marries Constanze Weber. The couple had six children, of whom only two survived infancy.
  • 1786 - Toward the end of the decade, Mozart's circumstances worsened.
  • 1789-90 - Mozart made some long journeys hoping to improve his fortunes, visiting Leipzig, Dresden, and Berlin in the spring of 1789, and Frankfurt, Mannheim, and other German cities in 1790.
  • 1791 - dies at the age of 35 in his home.
